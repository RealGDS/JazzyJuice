About KingSammelot

KingSammelot is a streamer and content creator from the United States. He is a multi-game streamer, showcasing various games like Minecraft and Fortnite, but his content largely revolves around Geometry Dash videos, stream highlights, and music.

With his engaging and entertaining style, KingSammelot has gained a reputation as one of the most captivating modern content creators within the Geometry Dash community.

KingSammelot has been actively playing Geometry Dash since 2014 but only started creating Geometry Dash content in 2021 after completing Bloodbath. Since then, he has amassed a dedicated following, surpassing over 100,000 subscribers on his YouTube channel.

In recognition of his talent and dedication, KingSammelot was honored with the Hidden Gem Award at the 2023 Streamer Awards, which celebrates streamers with fewer than 100 average viewers per stream.

Additionally, KingSammelot achieved significant milestones on his YouTube journey, reaching 10,000 subscribers on April 5, 2022, 50,000 subscribers on December 14, 2022, and crossing the 100,000 subscriber mark on February 27, 2023.

KingSammelot's skills extend beyond Geometry Dash, as he emerged victorious in a Minecraft event hosted by the renowned British Minecraft player Tubbo on March 22, 2023.

KingSammelot also has a significant presence on TikTok, amassing nearly 20,000 followers through his captivating content.
